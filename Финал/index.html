What is fasion???

Fashionable clothing is clothing that corresponds to the latest trends and fashion trends. It may vary for different age groups and different social groups. Fashionable clothing reflects modern trends, new ideas, and concepts, conveying them through style, material, color, and visual design. Each season, new collections of fashion designers appear, presenting different styles of clothing from classic to avant-garde. Currently, fashion has become very dynamic, and new directions and trends appear both on the streets of major cities and at major fashion shows. Fashionable clothing is created to allow each person to express themselves and their individual style. This can be seen as either a way of belonging to a certain group or expressing individuality and originality. In general, fashionable clothing is not just a functional element of a wardrobe, but also an expression of modern culture, its history, and trends. Each person can choose their style and be trendy, while still not forgetting their individuality and comfort.
